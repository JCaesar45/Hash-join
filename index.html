THE ARCHITECTURE OF EFFICIENCY

The hash join algorithm represents one of the most elegant solutions in relational algebra. By building an in-memory hash table (multimap) on the smaller relation during the build phase, we achieve constant-time probe lookups in the subsequent scan phase. This yields near-linear performance even on massive datasets.

Build: O(|B|)
Probe: O(|A| + |matches|)
Space: O(|B|)
